Our Vision

To create a vibrant and inclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem at SIT Hyderabad that nurtures innovation, enables start-ups, and empowers students, faculty, and alumni to become visionary entrepreneurs and change makers.

Our Mission

To inspire and support entrepreneurial aspirations, institutionalize innovation as a core value, enable incubation and funding for impactful startups, and foster interdisciplinary and global collaboration.
